Snake PVP

For my 2nd year, I was assigned a Team Project as one of my modules, worth 20 credits. This project involved creating a game in Java with the help of 4 other students in my cohort. We were examined on the game's Artificial Intelligence, Networking, Game Mechanics, User Interface, Audio as well as Team working skills, time management and efficiency. At the end of the module, we achieved 82% which we are proud of. To view our report and slides, click here. To play our game, please use this link.
